About Us

AnimeSearch Ver. 0.1 released in August 2022, AnimeSearch is an open-source search engine for all things anime. Currently, we have support for over 11k+ animes using data aggregated from various databases. The eventual goal of AnimeSearch is to be able to predict and recommend animes based on user preferences and their watch trends. AnimeSearch Ver. 0.1 has basic recommendation functionalities based on an interconnected relationship between all of the animes inside our database. More features are to come.
